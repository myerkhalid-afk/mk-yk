Food note

Ravioli mission

If Yusma wants ravioli, make El Brellin the pasta night and ask what stuffed pasta is on that evening. If they have the shrimp/pumpkin/burrata ravioli, that is the order.

Culture note

La Scala?

Carmen is at 20:00 on Sat 20 and Mon 22, but both conflict with booked dinners. Ballet starts after you leave. Recommendation: skip unless replacing a dinner.
